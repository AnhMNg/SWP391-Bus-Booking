Popular Routes

Ho Chi Minh City - Da Lat

Residents and tourists have said that Da Lat has all four seasons in one day: spring in the morning, summer at noon, autumn in the afternoon and winter at night, from the sunset to the sunrise.

Da Nang - Hue

Its status as transportation hub for central Vietnam and its proximity to several UNESCO World Heritage Sites, including the Imperial City of Hue, the Old Town of Hoi An, My Son ruins fuels much of its tourist activity.

Ha Noi - Sa Pa

The landscape itself is reason enough to make the 7-hour trek from Hanoi, but this place is much more than just a beautiful landscape. This mountainous town is home to five ethnic minority hill tribes.

Ho Chi Minh City - Can Tho

Cai Rang floating market. The largest floating marketing in Can Tho, and one of the most popular in the world. Cai Rang floating market was recognized as a national intangible cultural heritage.
